How It Works
Credits are flexible units of production.

Instead of fixed content quotas, each month you receive a set number of Credits to spend however your business needs. Shoot more one month, edit more the next. Your feed stays consistent regardless.

The Idea
One Credit, endless options.
A Credit is a unit of production output. You use it for whatever your content calendar needs that month. Video, photo, graphics, or on-site shooting.

Big shoot month ahead? Cash in Credits for media hours and video edits. Quiet month? Save them for carousels and graphics. You decide.
