Good to Know

The details.

Common questions about how we work, billing, and what to expect.

Simple Billing

SMM retainers billed on the 1st of each month. Sprints are 50% to reserve, 50% before the shoot day. Invoice, Zelle, PayPal, or bank transfer.

What You Receive

Finished deliverables with full rights to use them across social, web, and ads. Raw footage and extra edits available anytime as an add-on.

Local to You

Based in Orlando, serving Central Florida. Shoots within a 50-mile radius are included. Travel beyond is billed at cost.

Onboarding

SMM retainers include a one-time onboarding period where we set up your strategy, content calendar, and AI Architects stack. Sprints require no onboarding, we plan on the call.

Add-Ons Available

Ad management, additional platforms, extended community hours, bonus shoot days, and rush turnaround are available as add-ons on calls.

Clear Terms

SMM retainers begin with a 3-month minimum and continue month-to-month. Notice for termination is discussed on your call. Sprints are one-time, no ongoing obligation.
